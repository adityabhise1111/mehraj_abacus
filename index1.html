Abacus education is a mental arithmetic system that teaches children to perform calculations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, etc.) using a physical abacus initially, and then mentally by visualizing the abacus.

Benefits include improved concentration, enhanced memory, stronger mental calculation abilities, better problem-solving skills, and overall brain development, leading to academic excellence.
